Common questions

Good questions. Straight answers.

How much does landscaping cost?

It depends on the scope. A basic lawn maintenance plan starts around $45 per visit. Larger projects like patio installation or full landscape designs typically range from $3,000 to $25,000 depending on size and materials. We provide free estimates with transparent pricing so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.

What areas do you serve?

We serve homeowners across Northwest Arkansas including Bentonville, Rogers, Bella Vista, Centerton, Lowell, Cave Springs, Pea Ridge, Springdale, Fayetteville, and surrounding communities within about 25 miles of Bentonville.

How long does a typical project take?

Maintenance visits usually take 30 minutes to an hour. Smaller projects like mulching or sod installation are done in a day or two. Larger projects like patio construction or full landscape installations can take one to three weeks depending on complexity and weather.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes. We offer weekly and biweekly maintenance plans that include mowing, edging, trimming, and blowing. We also offer seasonal packages that add mulching, cleanups, and fertilization. Most of our maintenance clients have been with us for years.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Greenridge Landscaping is fully licensed and insured in the state of Arkansas. We carry general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage for every crew member on your property.

When is the best time to start a landscaping project?

In Northwest Arkansas, spring (March through May) and fall (September through November) are ideal for most plantings and installations. But we work year round. Hardscaping projects like patios and retaining walls can happen in most weather conditions. Call us anytime and we'll help you figure out the best timing.
